The Studio

Porous
Delab
Architecture  ·  Design  ·  Visualization  ·  Karachi

I founded Porous Delab on a simple conviction: that great spaces are porous — open to light, culture, memory, and the lives of those who inhabit them.

Under the studio, I work across four disciplines — architecture, interior design, 3D visualization, and landscaping — crafting environments that are both rigorously considered and deeply human. Each project begins not with a brief, but with a question: what does this place want to become?

Based in Karachi, I collaborate with clients across Pakistan and internationally, bringing the same commitment to craft whether the scale is a private residence or a civic landmark.

03.

The Practice

Maaz Khan is an architectural designer based in Karachi and the founder of Porous Delab. His work sits at the intersection of concept, space, and construction — driven by a conviction that clarity of design and thoughtful execution are inseparable.

Working across residential and commercial projects, he stays involved from the earliest design stages through to site — using visualization not as a presentation tool, but as a means to test ideas, resolve problems, and communicate intent.

Context, critical thinking, and the practical realities of building are not constraints to his process — they are the process.
